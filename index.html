Workshops

Our workshops are valuable as they are taught by professionals with years of real world project experience. We show what really works in our workshops so you can immediately apply the learning to your projects.

Our workshop offerings include:

  • Working With Business Processes
  • Data Modeling
  • Agile Team Training Scrum/Kanban
  • Agile Leadership
  • Agile Story Design
  • Visual Facilitation

All together the workshops provide an integrated set of techniques that can be applied to any large project. The workshops are highly compatible with Agile methods as they are best used for collaborative learning.

We read the manifesto and designed the workshops for Customer collaboration over contract negotation!

Coaching

Our coaching has been built up over the years of working with software delivery teams. We back our change visions with hands-on project experience. Being life-long learners with developed networks we have the capability to tailor specific training to help your team overcome their problems.

Our coaching offering inclues:

  • Agile Adoption for Software Teams
  • Requirements Facilitation for Analyst Teams

The goal of coaching is to advance your team to the point they have learned the new habits and no longer have a dependency on the coach. Our jobs is done when the team has internalized the new working experiences and updated their core beliefs to the new working model.

Consulting

  • Application selection
  • Support the adoption of new applications
